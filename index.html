Delicious Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients until just mixed.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
  8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
